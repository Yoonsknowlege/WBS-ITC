Lens.org Query Provenance — ISRU Construction Patent Dataset
=============================================================

This file documents the descriptive query provenance, including the
CPC anchors, Boolean keyword groups, supplementary targeted queries,
and post-retrieval processing steps used to assemble the released
analytical layer. It is **not** a verbatim Lens.org saved-search URL,
because Lens.org saved-search states are not guaranteed to replay
deterministically over time (index coverage and field semantics may
change). The query elements documented here are sufficient to
re-execute the same conceptual retrieval against Lens.org or another
patent search platform; the released 453-family analytical layer
itself is provided in `data/phase2_453_families.json` for downstream
reconstruction.

Search platform:  Lens.org (scholarly + patent search)
Retrieval date:   2026-03-28
Export format:    JSON records with Lens simple-family identifiers
                  retained at the record level. All downstream analyses
                  were consolidated to the Lens simple-patent-family
                  level.
Application year / priority year:
                  No date restriction was imposed at the retrieval-
                  population level. The released dataset records
                  earliest-priority years and publication years as
                  observed in the exported Lens family records; body
                  Fig. 3 reports earliest-priority-year tag intensity.
                  Retrieval was conducted on 2026-03-28; exact replay
                  of the Lens.org saved-search state is not claimed.

Retrieval strategy
------------------
The initial retrieval universe was constructed through a two-stage
CPC-based + keyword-expanded query on Lens.org.

Query component 1 -- CPC-anchored retrieval:
  CPC prefixes used in primary search:
    B64G (cosmonautic vehicles/equipment)
    C04B (lime; magnesia; slag; cements; ceramics; refractories)
    B33Y (additive manufacturing)
    E04H (buildings for particular purposes; shelters)
    B22F (working metallic powder)
    B28B (shaping clay or other ceramic compositions)
    G01N (investigating/analysing materials)
    B25J (manipulators; robots)
    E04B (general building constructions)

Query component 2 -- Keyword expansion:
  Boolean structure:
    ("lunar" OR "regolith" OR "planetary" OR "extraterrestrial"
     OR "moon" OR "mars" OR "space construction" OR "in-situ resource")
    AND
    ("construction" OR "manufacturing" OR "additive" OR "structure"
     OR "material" OR "sintering" OR "habitat" OR "shielding"
     OR "3d print" OR "robotic" OR "landing pad")

  Additional targeted queries for under-represented domains:
    "contour crafting" OR "lunar concrete" OR "regolith processing"
    "extraterrestrial construction" OR "space manufacturing"

Post-retrieval processing
-------------------------
1. Deduplication at the simple-family level
2. Two-pass author relevance screening with consensus scoring on a 0-5
   mechanism-level transfer-relevance rubric (retention threshold >= 2.5);
   no AI-generated ranking was used to exclude records.
   See manuscript Sections 3.1-3.3 and Online Resource 1 Appendix S2
   for the full screening protocol and retrieval flow record counts.
3. Versioned-codebook supplementary CPC-bridging rescue pass to identify
   families not captured in the initial pass (12 rescued records pre-dedup; 10 independent
   rescue families in the final 453-family released corpus, 9 of which
   are ITC-classified).

Retrieval flow record counts (matches Online Resource 1 Appendix S2)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 1: Initial Lens.org retrieval                     1,093 records
Stage 2: Two-pass author relevance screening (>=2.5)      511 records
Stage 3: Versioned-codebook ITC rescue                    +12 records
Stage 4: Pre-deduplication corpus                          523 records
Stage 5: Lens Simple Patent Family deduplication           453 unique families
Stage 6: Status-flag exclusions (9 false + 1 screening-log-only)   -10 families
Stage 7: Analytically active layer                          443 families
Stage 8: ITC-classified subset (>=1 assignment tag)        373 families
Stage 9: Assignment family-domain tags                     795 tags

Important limitations
---------------------
- The analytical dataset is NOT a direct Boolean output.
- The final 453-family released dataset was constructed through staged
  screening, author claim review, and supplementary retrieval.
- Exact retrieval-universe replay requires the Lens.org saved search
  state at the retrieval date, which may not be reproducible due to
  index updates and coverage changes.
- The repository supports framework reuse and analytical reconstruction,
  not deterministic replay of the initial retrieval universe.
